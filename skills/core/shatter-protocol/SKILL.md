# Shatter Protocol v0.2 🐸 (v3.0)

**Core Alignment**: Evidence-first, OPSEC-hardened, fully replicable framework with strong governance and auditability standards.

## Purpose
Shatter Protocol is a cross-cutting Layer-0 pre-filter designed to audit and break illusion layers in prompts, outputs, and narrative framing. It operationalizes powerful metaphors (mirror, flower, water, moon, Kaku system prompt spell, prime/composite illusion) into practical, repeatable gates that force visibility of what is being hidden, softened, or protected.

This is a mandatory component for high-stakes prompt hygiene, AI output auditing, and any work where truth risks being filtered by corporate safety layers, narrative comfort, or institutional framing.

## v3.0 Alignment (Mandatory)
- **Layer-0 Pre-Filter**: Non-bypassable on all high-stakes, recurrent-depth, or narrative-sensitive work. Must be invoked **before** Sovereign Lens routing or final synthesis on Tier 1 tasks.
- **4-Agent Orchestration Default**: Agent 3 (Verification & Integrity) is the primary consumer. Agent 1 ensures Gate 0 passage and logs vetoes.
- **Working Document Persistence**: All Shatter runs, gate outcomes, and identified illusion layers must be logged to working-doc-manager with tri-state results and escalation notes.
- **Tri-State Evidence (+1 / 0 / -1)**: Each gate produces explicit tri-state on whether the illusion was successfully shattered or remains partially intact.
- **Governance Enforcement**: Any detected softening, hedging that protects institutional comfort, or refusal to surface base truth triggers immediate escalation.

## Core Protocol — 7-Gate Truth-Shatter Process

**Gate 0 — Entry Gate** (Non-bypassable)
- Confirm Layer-0 readiness (evidence-gate + shatter-protocol invocation).

**Gates 1–6 — Illusion Shatter Gates** (Mirror / Flower / Water / Moon / Prime / Composite)
1. **Mirror Gate** — What is being reflected back versus what is actually present?
2. **Flower Gate** — What is blooming (visible truth) versus what is being cultivated to distract?
3. **Water Gate** — What flows naturally versus what is being dammed or redirected?
4. **Moon Gate** — What is illuminated versus what remains in shadow?
5. **Prime Gate** — What is fundamental versus what is derivative or constructed?
6. **Composite Gate** — What appears whole but is actually assembled from fragments?

**Gate 7 — Integration & Output Gate**
- Summarize shattered illusions, remaining intact layers, and recommended rewrites.
