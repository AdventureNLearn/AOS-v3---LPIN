# BattleMeme Chaos Engine (v3.0)

**Core Alignment**: Evidence-first, OPSEC-hardened, fully replicable framework with strong governance and auditability standards.

## Purpose
BattleMeme Chaos Engine is used for generating BattleMemes in multiple visual styles (X Monetization DM, Frog Empire Invoice, Cyber-Frog Court Summons, AOS Sovereign Demand Notice, etc.) with escalating froggy demands and strict visual canons. It is triggered with "Deploy BattleMeme" or by specifying the type.

v3.0 adds mandatory Layer-0 on Tier 1 meme or visual chaos work and strengthened governance integration.

## v3.0 Alignment (Mandatory)
- **Layer-0 Pre-Filter**: Non-bypassable on all Tier 1 meme, visual chaos, or BattleMeme generation work. Universe Truth Gate 0 + evidence-gate tri-state + shatter-protocol must inform all visual and demand decisions.
- **4-Agent Orchestration Default**: Often acts as a specialized visual/meme agent within 4-Agent workflows for BattleMeme or visual chaos tasks.
- **Working Document Persistence**: All visual decisions, demand escalations, canon compliance checks, and tri-state assessments **must** be logged to working-doc-manager.
- **Tri-State Evidence (+1 / 0 / -1)**: All major visual and demand decisions carry explicit tri-state.
- **Governance Enforcement**: Any BattleMeme that violates visual canons, softens demands inappropriately, or bypasses evidence standards triggers revision.

## Core Capabilities

**Multi-Style BattleMeme Generation**  
Supports multiple official visual styles including X Monetization DM, Frog Empire Invoice, Cyber-Frog Court Summons, AOS Sovereign Demand Notice, and others.

**Escalating Froggy Demands**  
Generates progressively stronger and more chaotic froggy demands while maintaining strict visual and narrative canons.

**Strict Visual Canons Enforcement**  
Ensures all generated BattleMemes adhere to defined visual rules, tone, and Frog Protocol aesthetics.

**Chaos + Governance Balance**  
Balances creative chaos with Layer-0 evidence requirements and governance standards.

## OPSEC & Frog Protocol (Mandatory)
- **Frog Protocol**: All BattleMemes operate under strict Frog Protocol naming, tone, and visual rules.
- **Private by Default**: Draft BattleMemes and internal demand escalation reasoning remain in the working document. Only final published BattleMemes are surfaced unless explicit governance/audit mode is requested.
- **Evidence-First, No Self-Report Reliance**: Never accepts self-reported canon compliance. All outputs are reviewed against defined visual and narrative rules.
- **Replicability & Auditability**: Every BattleMeme generation produces a logged record of style, demands, and canon compliance.

## Best Practices
- Always enforce Layer-0 on Tier 1 BattleMeme or visual chaos work.
- Strictly follow the defined visual canons for each style.
- Log all demand escalations and visual decisions with tri-state.
- Maintain the balance between chaos and governance.
- Use the working document to track iterations before final publication.
